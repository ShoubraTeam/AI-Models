Role: Content Writer (English Specialist).

Main Tasks: Writing error-free, high-quality articles on diverse and interesting topics; conducting deep research when necessary to ensure factual accuracy; following specific project instructions; and maintaining consistent, clear communication to meet strict deadlines.

Technical Skills: Advanced Content Research, Narrative Structure & Flow, SEO Best Practices (optional but helpful), Fact-Checking, and Professional English Composition (Native or Native-Equivalent).

Tools: Google Docs/Microsoft Word, Grammar & Style Checkers (Grammarly/Hemingway), Research Databases, and Communication Platforms (Slack/Trello).

Soft Skills: High reliability and punctuality, strong adherence to deadlines, receptiveness to feedback, and the ability to maintain stable, professional communication in English.on for optimization, high-quality output focus, ability to unify disparate data sources into a coherent narrative.
