Role: Product Photo Editor (DTC & E-commerce Retouching).

Main Tasks: Processing RAW files to project specifications, performing advanced color correction and tonal balancing across product lines, creating clean white-background listings with natural shadows, retouching physical imperfections (dust, scratches, reflections), and exporting optimized assets for Shopify, Amazon, and social media.

Technical Skills: Advanced Photo Retouching & Compositing, Color Grading & Consistency, RAW Image Processing, High-End Product Masking, and AI-Augmented Editing Workflows (Background Cleanup/Prop Placement).

Tools: Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Lightroom, and AI-enhanced editing tools. Bonus: Graphic Design for Infographics and E-commerce Platform Optimization (Shopify/Amazon).

Soft Skills: Obsessive attention to detail, proactive communication, reliable fast turnaround times, and a commitment to realism over automated/artificial-looking results.
