Role: Creative Graphic Designer (Brand, Web & Social focus).

Main Tasks: Creating high-quality graphics for brand assets, websites, and social media; assisting with logo concepts and visual identity systems; designing UX-driven website elements (hero sections, icons, infographics); collaborating on wireframes for Webflow/WordPress; and developing engaging content for LinkedIn, Instagram, and TikTok.

Technical Skills: Visual Identity System Design, UX/UI Layout & Wireframing, Infographic Creation, Typography Alignment, Social Media Asset Production, and Web-Optimized Graphic Design.

Tools: Adobe Creative Suite (Illustrator/Photoshop), Figma (for UX/UI support), Canva, and familiarity with Webflow/WordPress environments.

Soft Skills: Intentional and detail-oriented execution, "solutions-forward" attitude, ability to take and implement direct feedback, high-speed collaboration, and a hunger for continuous creative growth.
