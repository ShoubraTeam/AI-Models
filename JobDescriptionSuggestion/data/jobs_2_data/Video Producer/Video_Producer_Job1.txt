Role: Video Production Specialist (Multi-Platform Content Creator).

Main Tasks: Conceptualizing and storyboarding video ideas, shooting high-quality raw footage, and editing dynamic videos tailored for specific platform algorithms (YouTube vs. Instagram). You will be responsible for the end-to-end production process to ensure all content aligns with the brand’s visual identity.

Technical Skills: End-to-End Video Production, Narrative Storytelling & Pacing, Color Grading & Correction, Sound Design & Audio Mixing, and Platform-Specific Formatting (Vertical vs. Horizontal).

Tools: Adobe Premiere Pro, Final Cut Pro, or DaVinci Resolve; After Effects (for basic motion graphics); and professional camera/lighting equipment.

Soft Skills: Creative conceptualization, strong attention to detail, passion for social media trends, and the ability to translate brand vision into compelling visual content.
