Role: Motion Graphics Artist & Video Producer (SaaS & AI Automation focus).

Main Tasks: Executing high-quality product and marketing videos based on internal briefs, rapidly producing video templates and tutorials, participating in creative brainstorming sessions to translate ideas into production tasks, and maintaining a high standard of visual storytelling and timing.

Technical Skills: Advanced Motion Design (After Effects), Product/SaaS Visualization, Rapid Prototyping/Templating, Visual Composition & Layout, and Storyboard-to-Video Execution. Bonus: AE Scripting or ExtendScript experience for workflow automation.

Tools: Adobe After Effects (Expert), Premiere Pro, and Adobe Creative Suite. Familiarity with AI video/content tools is a significant plus.

Soft Skills: High execution speed, strong visual judgment, collaborative brainstorming mindset, and a "growth-focused" attitude toward emerging technologies.
