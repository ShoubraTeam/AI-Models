Role: Video Production Specialist (Scripting & Editing focus).

Main Tasks: Developing engaging scripts that align with brand objectives, executing the end-to-end video editing process, ensuring a professionally polished final product, and utilizing creative storytelling techniques to produce compelling visual content.

Technical Skills: Creative Scriptwriting, Advanced Video Editing & Post-Production, Narrative Storytelling, Brand-Aligned Visual Design, and High-Fidelity Rendering.

Tools: Adobe Premiere Pro, DaVinci Resolve, or Final Cut Pro; Scripting software; and Motion Graphics tools.

Soft Skills: Strong creative vision, attention to detail, ability to translate abstract ideas into visuals, and proactive collaboration to meet brand standards.
