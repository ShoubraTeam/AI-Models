Role: Motion Graphics Designer & Product Video Specialist.

Main Tasks: Translating existing Canva storyboards and details into a high-fidelity 1-minute video; designing custom, premium backgrounds and environments; implementing complex UI/UX animations; and executing smooth, high-energy transitions that effectively showcase the product's value proposition.

Technical Skills: Advanced Motion Graphics (2D/3D), UI/UX Product Animation, Kinetic Typography, Dynamic Transitions, and High-End Visual Storytelling.

Tools: Adobe After Effects (Primary), Cinema 4D or Blender (for 3D depth), Adobe Premiere Pro, and Figma/Canva (for asset extraction).

Soft Skills: High level of creative initiative, ability to replicate premium "SaaS-style" aesthetics, strong attention to detail, and the ability to take conceptual direction and "elevate" it into a polished final product.
