Role: SaaS Video Producer (Software Demo & Feature Specialist).

Main Tasks: Writing concise, benefit-driven scripts for software walkthroughs; recording high-quality screen captures and UI interactions; editing videos to highlight key workflows; and incorporating animations or motion graphics to emphasize specific features and data points.

Technical Skills: Screen Recording & Editing, Software UI Animation, Scriptwriting for Technical Products, Motion Graphics (Callouts/Lower Thirds), and Narrative Pacing for SaaS.

Tools: Adobe Premiere Pro or Camtasia; Adobe After Effects (for UI animations); Screen capture software (Loom/ScreenFlow); and Voiceover/Audio editing tools.

Soft Skills: Keen eye for detail, ability to simplify complex software concepts for a general audience, passion for tech storytelling, and the ability to work independently from concept to final export.
