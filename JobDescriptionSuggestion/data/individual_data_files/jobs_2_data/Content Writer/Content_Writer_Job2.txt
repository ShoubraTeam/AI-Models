Role: YouTube Script Writer (Specialized in History).

Main Tasks: Conducting thorough, non-superficial research (academic sources), writing scripts for 10-15 minute videos (1,800-2,500 words), and structuring each video into at least 10 high-value talking points.

Technical Skills: Narrative Structuring, Historical Research, Academic Sourcing (Non-Wikipedia), Audience Hooking/Engagement, Scriptwriting for Voiceovers.

Tools: Google Docs.

Soft Skills: Passion for history, natural conversational tone, reliable turnaround times, ability to maintain viewer interest throughout long-form content.
