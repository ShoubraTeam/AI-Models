Role: Data Analyst.

Main Tasks: Collecting and processing large datasets, identifying trends, and creating visualizations to support strategic planning and performance improvement.

Technical Skills: Data Processing, Trend Analysis, Statistical Reporting, Data Cleaning, Strategic Planning Support.

Tools: SQL, Advanced Excel, Data Visualization software (e.g., Tableau, Power BI).

Soft Skills: Proactivity, clear communication of insights to non-technical stakeholders, organizational skills, and the ability to work independently or within a team.
