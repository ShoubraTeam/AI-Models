Role: Contract Data Analyst (Reporting to CFO).

Main Tasks: Transforming raw financial and operational data into actionable insights, building automated dashboards from scratch, and integrating data from multiple platforms (Shopify, QBO, TripleWhale).

Technical Skills: Data Modeling, Advanced Excel, SQL, Business Intelligence (BI) Development, Data Governance & Integrity, Financial/Margin Analysis.

Tools: Power BI/Tableau/Looker, SQL, Shopify, QuickBooks Online (QBO), TripleWhale, Gorgias.

Soft Skills: Systems thinking, business acumen, ability to communicate insights to leadership, and the ability to build frameworks in a fast-paced environment.
