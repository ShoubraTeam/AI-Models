Role: Graphic Designer (Video Overlay & Social Assets Specialist).

Main Tasks: Designing branded PNG overlays for video integration, creating custom lower-thirds and call-to-action graphics, ensuring all assets are optimized for various social media aspect ratios, and collaborating on video production workflows to ensure seamless asset application.

Technical Skills: Motion-Aware Graphic Design, Transparency/Alpha Channel Optimization (PNG), Branding & Visual Identity, and Social Media Video Specifications.

Tools: Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Photoshop, and familiarity with Video Editing software (Premiere Pro/After Effects/CapCut).

Soft Skills: Strong attention to detail, ability to translate brand identity into video-compatible assets, efficient project management for short-term deadlines, and proactive communication.
