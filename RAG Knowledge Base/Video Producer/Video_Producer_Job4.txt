Role: Whiteboard Animator / Explainer Video Specialist.

Main Tasks: Executing a 2:30 minute whiteboard animation based on a provided storyboard and voiceover, translating complex AI business concepts into clear and engaging hand-drawn or digital-sketch visuals, and ensuring smooth pacing and synchronization with the audio track.

Technical Skills: Whiteboard Animation (2D), Visual Metaphor Development, Sync-to-Voiceover Editing, Character & Icon Illustration, and Complex Information Visualization.

Tools: VideoScribe, Doodly, Vyond, or Adobe Animate/After Effects (with custom drawing paths).

Soft Skills: Strong attention to detail, ability to grasp and visualize complex technical/AI processes, commitment to deadlines, and excellent communication for feedback implementation.
