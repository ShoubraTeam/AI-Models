Role: Podcast & Video Production Specialist (Multi-Platform Content Focus).

Main Tasks: Editing raw audio for clarity (removing fillers/noise), mixing and mastering episodes, editing full-length video episodes for YouTube, and repurposing content into high-engagement short-form clips (Reels, TikTok, Shorts). You will also handle distribution tasks like writing show notes, timestamps, and formatting uploads.

Technical Skills: Professional Audio Mixing & Mastering, Multi-Camera Video Editing, Short-Form Content Optimization (Hooks/Pacing), YouTube SEO & Metadata Formatting, and Digital Asset Management.

Tools: Adobe Premiere Pro, Final Cut Pro, or CapCut; Adobe Audition (or similar audio workstations); Podcast Hosting Platforms; and basic Graphic Design tools (Canva/Photoshop).

Soft Skills: Strong attention to detail, strategic storytelling mindset, ability to work independently to meet deadlines, and a collaborative approach to creative brainstorming.
