Role: Video Producer (Non-Profit & Event Presentation focus).

Main Tasks: Producing a long-form event video that highlights website features and mission impact, conceptualizing a narrative that resonates with a formal audience, integrating existing hero banner footage into a cohesive story, and utilizing AI explainer tools to clarify the foundation's educational objectives.

Technical Skills: Event-Scale Video Production, Narrative Scriptwriting & Storyboarding, Motion Graphics (for website walkthroughs), AI-Assisted Explainer Video Creation, and Emotional Storytelling for the Non-Profit sector.

Tools: Adobe Premiere Pro or DaVinci Resolve, After Effects (for motion graphics), AI Voiceover/Explainer tools, and High-Quality Sound Design libraries.

Soft Skills: Deep alignment with non-profit values, extreme attention to detail for formal presentations, creative vision, and the ability to produce content that drives engagement and donor resonance.
