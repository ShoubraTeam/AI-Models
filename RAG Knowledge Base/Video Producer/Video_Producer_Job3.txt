Role: Short-Form Ad Creator (eCommerce & Performance Creative focus).

Main Tasks: Developing simple ad concepts and scripts, sourcing footage (stock, social, or AI-generated), editing vertical-format videos with strong hooks and captions, and delivering ready-to-use creatives for platforms like TikTok, Meta, and Instagram.

Technical Skills: Performance-Driven Video Editing, Hook & Script Writing, On-Screen Text/Caption Styling, AI-Assisted Content Production (Voiceover/Visuals), and Social Media Ad Optimization.

Tools: Adobe Premiere Pro, CapCut, or After Effects; AI Tools (ElevenLabs, Midjourney, etc.); Stock Footage Libraries; and Voiceover/TTS software.

Soft Skills: Efficient production workflow, ability to translate product features into compelling hooks, responsiveness to testing feedback, and clear communication regarding creative variations.
