Main Tasks: Scouting and securing an excellent, professional location suitable for multi-person interviews/conversations; managing the full video recording process; ensuring high-quality audio capture for multiple speakers; and delivering polished footage optimized for corporate social media channels.

Technical Skills: Multi-Camera Setup Management, Studio/Location Lighting for Interviews, High-Fidelity Audio Recording (Lavalier/Boom setups), Location Scouting & Logistics, and Corporate Cinematography.

Tools: High-definition Mirrorless/Cinema Cameras, Professional Lighting Kits, Multi-channel Audio Recorders, and Video Editing Software (Premiere Pro/DaVinci Resolve).

Soft Skills: Strong professional communication, ability to identify "premium" aesthetics suitable for a consultancy firm, reliability in high-stakes corporate environments, and local logistical knowledge within Türkiye.
